Mrinmoyi

Date April 2023
Location Noapara, Chittagong
Client Mr. Ghosh
Category Building & Construction

........... "Mrinmoyi"..............
-means that it is of earth. “Life comes from the earth and returns to the earth”

Transitioning from the city to rural living is something many of us dream of. Swapping the hustle and bustle of regular life to days of peace and tranquility and releasing the soul from the huge gigantic concrete structures! In this project we wanted a simple earthy look. From tiles to wall colors there we tried to give an organic feel . We tried to provide maximum open spaces in both the interior and exterior of the building. Such as the porch. Listening to the birds in the morning and the crickets at night!
